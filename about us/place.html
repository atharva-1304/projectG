Important places in Mandir premises

Shree’s Samadhi Grahan Sthal (the holy place where Shree Maharaj merged himself with Supreme Bramha)

It is beside Shree’s Vishranti Sthal. On Bhadrapad Shuddha Panchami, Shake 1832 (8th September 1910) Shri Gajanan Maharaj concluded His incarnated life by Sanjeevan Samadhi. This place is co-mmemorated as Shree’s Samadhi Grahan Sthal. The idols of Shri Vithoba – Rukhumai are behind Shree’s Samadhi Garhan Sthal.Dhuni (the holy fire) ignited by Shree himself in those days (which are still in blaze) and pair of Forceps used by Shree are also near Shree’s Samadhi Grahan Sthal.

Lord Hanumant Mandir (Facing south)

Near Audumbar, temple of Lord Hanumant (Facing south) is located which is very ancient from the period of Shree’s stay.

Samadhi Mandir of Shri Balabhau Maharaj and Shri Narayan Maharaj:

Samadhi Mandir of the two most faithful and loyal volunteers of Shree Maharaj namely- Shri Balabhau Maharaj and Shri Narayan Maharaj is located near behind the Shree’s Mandir.

Shri Nagdevata Mandir

Shree used to take rest and reside here. Shri Nagdevata Mandir is in the northern part of Shree’s Mandir. Earlier this part was occupied by forest plants and trees. This is Shree’s holy and protective place in the northern part of Mandir premises

Shri Audumbar (Holy Tree)

The Audumbar is at the east of Shree’s Samadhi Grahan Sthal.Shree used to sit under it.

holy places in shegaon where shree visited

Shri Bankat Sadan:

Shree blessed his loyal devotee Bankatlal and enabled him to notice Shree’s appearing in Shegaon. ‘Shree’ resided here and gave the experiences of his incarnated life to many fortunate devotees in the same place. This holy place – ‘BankatSadan’ was later handed over to Sansthan by descendants of Bankatlalji. To maintain the spirituality of this holy place, Sansthan built here three storeyed building- ‘Smriti Bhavan’which is named as ‘Bankat Sadan’. Shree’s Paduka and photo are kept here with great faith and every day Puja and Aarti is performed at morning and evening.

Shri Maruti Mandir (Shri Sitalamata Mandir)

This is an ancient temple where ‘Shree’ spent much of the time. Shri Sitalamata Mandir Trust is also merged in Sansthan. This temple gained great spirituality due to Shree’s stay. It was renovated by Sansthan in Mable stone quite attractively. Shree’sGadi (Holy Bed) and Paduka are kept here. This is the same temple that witnessed the great omnipotent capacity of Maharaj when adamant youths from Patil family totally surrendered at Shree’s feet due to His great Yogic power.

The important shrines around Shegaon visited by Shree Shri Kshetra Nagzari:

Shri Kshetra Nagzari is situated, on the bank of Man River, five Kms away from Shegaon. Samadhi Mandir of Sant Gomaji Maharaj is an important place to visit here. The ancient temple of Shri Nageshwar and the beautiful flow of spiritual streams gave the identity as Shri Kshetra Nagzari to this village. Shri Gomaji Maharaj was devotee of Lord Krishna.
