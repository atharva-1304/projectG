Shri Kshetra Pandharpur

In the first branch of Shri Sansthan at Shri Khetra Pandharpur, the beautiful temple of Shri Gajanan Maharaj is built in marble stones in 8.5 acres of land where Shree Sansthan had the PranPratishtha of Shree's idol (with all religious rituals). Here the grand Mahadwar (Entrunce gate) 72 feet high and 51 feet wide built in Dholpuri stone attracts the devotees.

The important facilities made available by shegaon Sansthan at Pandharpur
  • 5 Bhakt Niwas coplex with 252 rooms
  • Homeopathy clinic with medicines.
  • Warkari Shikshan Sanstha.
  • Sangeet Vidhyalaya (Music School for Bhajan, Pravachan & Kirtan)
  • Two Community halls.
  • Mobile Dispensary for week (during Ashadhi & Kartiki Ekadashi Utsav) about 20 to 25 Thousand devotees facilited yearly.
  • Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner facility and Sabudana Khichadi, Bhagar, Aamati& Banana distributed at Ekadhashi moment.
  • Free of cost food (Rice and Kadhi) for devotees reaching, very late. (Timings 10.00 pm. to 1.00 am) at Sansthan's branch.
Shri Kshetra Traymbakeshwar

Devotees of Lord Shiva frequently rush at Shree Khetra Trymbakeshwar in Nashik. To facilitate them and for the welfare of tribals Shree Sansthan started its branch at Tryambakeshwar in 1995.

read more

The beautiful temple of Shri Gajanan Maharaj was built in marble stones and had the PranPratishtha of Shree's idol with all religious rituals. The grand Mahadwar built in dholpuri stone attracts the devotees, and enables them to have the glimpse of Shree's temple built in beautiful marble stone, with calm and spiritual atmosphere, which appeals the devotees at once. Here Puja, Aarti and Abhishek is performed by the priests and Bhajan is organised on Thursday, Chaturdashi and Ekadashi. Parayan, Haripath and Mahaprasad distribution is made on the auspicious occasion of Shree Pragat Din.

The Important facilites made available by Shree Sansthan at Tryambakeshwar
  • 4 Bhakta Niwas Complex with 177 rooms
  • Alternate arrangment of 300 beds.
  • Additional Hall for accomodation
  • Parking facility
  • Mobile dispensary for tribals
  • Clothes and Mahaprasad with sweets for tribals during Diwali festival (for around 25000 tribals)
  • Distribution of Bhajani Sasitya to Dindis.
Shri Kshetra Omkareshwar

Shri Kshetra Omkareshwar is situated on the bank of the holy river Narmada in Madhya Pradesh. It is one of the twelve Jotirlinga in India. At Shri Kshetra Omkareshwar, Shree Sansthan started its branch where a beutiful temple of Shri Gajanan Maharaj is built in marble stones in 4 acres of land. Shree's Idol'sPranpratitshtha was accomplished with all devotion and religious rituals. The grand Mahadwar built in dholpuri stone attractes the devotees. Here are 4 Bhakta Niwas having 104 room with 200 additional bed system

Shri Kshetra Girada

Just like Kshri Kshetra Kapildhara, Girda is eqully tranquil and the most befitting place for meditatino wchich is situated in the arms of nature. Hence Shree Sansthan started and recongnised its branch at Girda as- Sadhana Sthala. On the basis of Anand Sagar, Dhyan kendra (medidation hall) is under construction. Girda is quite suitable for meditation for sadhus of all religiouns. Tapashvi Niwas (Accomodation for devotees coming for meditation) with all facilities is under construction. Shri Kshetra Alandi

Shri Kshetra Alandi of Sant Dnyaneshwar Maharaj is yet another holy place that attracts the devotees of Lord Vithoba. To facilitate the devotees coming for darshan of Shri Dnyaneshwar Mauli and to pramote Bhagwat Dhwarma Shree Sansthan states its branch at Alandi. In 1998, Sansthan started the construction of three buildings Shree's temple's construction will be completed within 02 years,while the grand entrance gate built in dholpuri stone with beautiful carvings is already completed. Presently a Pravachan hall facilitates the devotees for parayan ad Harinam.
