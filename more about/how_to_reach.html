How to Reach

In Maharashtra State, the holy town Shegaon is in Buldhana district located 16 kms away from NH-6 (Via-Khamgaon). Nearrest airport are Nagpur and Aurangabad at 5 hours distrance. Itis well connected to almost all the places by road, railways and by air. Shegaon is A-class station of central railway located on Mumbai- Nagpur track where almost all the trains have halt at shegaon (with the exception of superfast trains like Okhapuri, Prerana and delux Dnyaneshwari). Shegaon is also well connected with Gujrat and Madhya Pradesh state by inter state bus service. Like this, so much cities and holy places of Maharashtra are connected with Shegaon. There is an enquiry booth in railway station premices from where devotees can take guidence. Bus stand is also located on the way of Shree Temple.

Air Route

Delhi - Mumbai - Aurangabad, Banglore- Mumbai-Aurangabad (nearestairport) Shegaon is 225 Kms. About 4hours trains journey from Nagpur

Delhi-Nagpur,Chennai-Nagpur and Kolkata- Nagpur.Shegaon is 300 Kms. About 6 hours by road or 4.30 hours trains journey from Nagpur.

Railway Route

Delhi- Bhusawal and Banglore- Bhusawal; Shegaon is 120 Kms. About 3 hours road or trains journey from Bhusawal

Mumbai- Bhusawal - Shegaon (towards Nagpur).

Kolkatta- Nagpur- Shegaon (towards Mumbai/Ahmedabad)

Chennai-Nagpur-Shegaon (towards Mumbai/Ahmedabad)

Road Route

Bhusawal - Shegaon(120 Kms 3 hours journey)

Aurangabad-Shegaon(225Kms 4 hours journey).

Nagpur-Shegaon(300 Kms 6 hours journey)
